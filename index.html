Data science for all

For a long time, statistical and digital methods were restricted to hard sciences. Fortunately, historians and humanists can now benefit from data science as well. This webpage provides programming lessons on the use of data science in digital humanities. Learn to conduct efficient statistical analyses and show your insights with beautiful visualizations.
